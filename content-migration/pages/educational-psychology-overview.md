Educational psychology input to ADHD diagnostic pathway, including cognitive testing, behavioral observation, and classroom strategies.

Assessment of cognitive and language profile, recommendations for differentiation and support (often integrated with SALT input).

## FAQs

**Q: How quickly can you provide an Educational Psychologist?**
A: We provide **CVs within 24 hours** of your enquiry. Assessment appointments can usually be arranged within 1-2 weeks depending on location and EP availability. For urgent EHCP cases approaching deadline, we prioritize accordingly.

**Q: Do your EPs work at local authority rates?**
A: We offer competitive pricing and can work with LA budgets. For volume contracts (MATs, LAs), we provide scaled pricing. Contact us for a tailored quote.

**Q: Can you help us meet 20-week EHCP timescales?**
A: Yes. Our EPs prioritize statutory assessment work and understand the 20-week countdown. We guarantee report delivery within agreed timescales (typically 4-6 weeks from assessment).

**Q: Are your Educational Psychologists HCPC registered?**
A: Yes. All our EPs are HCPC-registered with Practitioner Psychologist status. They hold professional indemnity insurance and enhanced DBS clearance.

**Q: Can you provide ongoing EP support for our MAT/schools?**
A: Yes. We can arrange block contracts with dedicated EPs who work consistently with your schools â€” building relationships, understanding context, and providing continuity. Flexible arrangements from half-day per week to termly input.

**Q: Do your EPs conduct in-person assessments?**
A: Yes. Our EPs conduct in-person assessments at schools, homes, or neutral venues. Remote assessment is also available for consultations and some screening tools, but comprehensive EHCP assessments typically require face-to-face interaction.

**Q: What specialist areas do your EPs cover?**
A: Our network includes EPs with specialist expertise in: autism, dyslexia, ADHD, attachment & trauma, mental health, learning disability, speech & language difficulties, sensory processing, behavior, and complex needs. We match you with the right specialist for each case.
