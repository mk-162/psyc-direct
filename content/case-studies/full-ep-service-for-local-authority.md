## The Brief

A Council in Yorkshire was facing challenges in delivering the EP service to their schools that was needed. The organisation struggled with EP shortages and extended timescales within the EHCP process. They required support serving 115 schools needing statutory assessments for pupils with Special Educational Needs.

## Our Action

Psychology Direct identified required resource levels, then sourced Educational Psychologists from the network within the local area who had capacity and relevant experience. We established a booking system and fulfilment service through our Head of Education, along with structured communications including regular client reviews.

## The Outcome

The team managed approximately 15 assessments per month with associated administrative work handled entirely by Psychology Direct. We also accommodated additional requests, including an out-of-contact assessment. A 15-month contract followed, demonstrating client satisfaction.
