## The Brief

At Psychology Direct we are accustomed to working and delivering to tight timescales. However, this case required us to go the extra mile to meet an extraordinarily quick turnaround on a court ordered report. It had to be completed and submitted within one week.

## Our Action

Our Client Managers leveraged their extensive knowledge of the Expert Witness network to identify a professional known for flexibility and fast delivery. Rather than relying on email correspondence, they took a proactive personal approach and secured an expert within 20 minutes of the solicitor's inquiry. The assessment date was confirmed immediately, and the appointment was arranged directly with the defence solicitor to eliminate delays in the referral process.

## The Outcome

The assessment and report were completed within 24 hours. This was an incredible achievement, and down to the fast work of our client manager and the breadth of our network, among which we know we have Expert Witnesses skilled and willing to take on fast turnaround cases. The trial proceeded without costly postponements.

## Client Feedback

> "The Case Manager was amazed at our efficiency and ability, to not only meet, but exceed their challenging deadline."

In recognition of our reliable and efficient service, we have since been sent other requests from this client.
