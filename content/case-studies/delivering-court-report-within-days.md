## The Brief

During a Fitness to Plead assessment, the client became unresponsive to questions, threatening to delay the trial and render the report insufficient for court purposes. The solicitor firm urgently requested assistance securing a new assessment and report before the court's deadline.

## Our Action

The team moved immediately to understand the underlying issue. Manager Lewis contacted the previous psychologist for insights into the client's lack of responsiveness. Using this information and his extensive knowledge of the psychologist network, Lewis identified suitable expert witnesses within 24 hours. The selected professional could engage the client effectively and meet the submission deadline. Lewis managed the booking, liaison, and administrative support throughout.

## The Outcome

With the new psychologist engaged, the client became more cooperative. The second Fitness to Plead assessment produced more conclusive, reportable outcomes. The expert witness completed and submitted the report within the deadline, accompanied by quality assurance checks meeting the highest standards.

## Client Feedback

The firm submitted two additional inquiries within 24 hours of report submission, demonstrating their satisfaction with Psychology Direct's service delivery and responsiveness.
