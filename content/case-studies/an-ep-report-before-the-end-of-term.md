## The Brief

A Primary School's SENCO faced a deadline pressure: securing an Educational Psychology report for an EHCP before the final panel meeting of the academic year. With Educational Psychologists in short supply and many unavailable due to holiday schedules, the school reached out to Psychology Direct for urgent assistance.

## Our Action

Psychology Direct's Client Manager Ana responded immediately to the Friday call. She gathered case details and identified the required EP expertise and geographical needs. Working rapidly against a less-than-one-week deadline, Ana contacted qualified Educational Psychologists from the network to secure someone available. Throughout the process, she maintained communication with the SENCO and thoroughly briefed the assigned EP on the case background, enabling proper assessment preparation and time efficiency.

## The Outcome

The assessment occurred on Tuesday, with the completed report submitted Thursday — meeting the final panel meeting deadline. The combination of proactive client management and Psychology Direct's extensive EP network enabled delivery within the demanding timeframe, ultimately supporting the child and family requiring assessment and intervention.
