## The Brief

A challenging case requiring highly specialist Expert Witness testimony, delivered against moving parameters, and tight timescales. The solicitor needed niche expertise that was difficult to source, with a compressed three to four week timeline. An additional complication involved the defendant's prison transfers with unpredictable logistics.

## Our Action

The Client Manager identified the required specialist from Psychology Direct's network. With only two experts available in this field, urgent coordination was necessary. Contact was established within one hour rather than the standard 24 hours. The team coordinated assessment dates and locations, accounting for prison uncertainty. When the defendant transferred to a more distant facility, significant liaison occurred between Psychology Direct, the Expert Witness, and the solicitor to reorganise the assessment.

## The Outcome

The Expert Witness demonstrated flexibility by accepting the new location, offering revised dates, and maintaining commitment to the original timescale. The team's collaborative effort enabled the solicitor to submit the report to court successfully, ensuring proper legal representation for the defendant.
