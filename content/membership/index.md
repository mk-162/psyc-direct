# Why Become a Member?

There's a fundamental difference between seeing a doctor when you're sick and having a team that knows your health inside and out. Between rushing through a ten-minute appointment and having time to really talk. Between wondering if something's wrong and knowing you're being monitored proactively.

Membership at Cocoon is about building a genuine partnership in your health. It's about having practitioners who know your history, understand your goals, and are there when you need them. It's about the peace of mind that comes from knowing your health is in good hands.

## Exclusive Benefits

Membership unlocks a level of care and access that goes beyond individual appointments. From priority booking to exclusive events, being a Cocoon member means being part of something more.

But the real benefit isn't any single feature — it's the cumulative effect of having a team that truly knows you. Of catching issues early because someone's paying attention. Of having someone to turn to when you have questions or concerns. Of feeling like your health matters.

## Priority Access

Same-week appointments. Extended consultation times. Direct contact with your care team. When you need us, we're here.

We've all experienced the frustration of waiting weeks for a doctor's appointment, only to feel rushed through a ten-minute consultation. As a Cocoon member, those days are behind you. Our priority booking system means you'll typically see a practitioner within a week — often within 48 hours for Complete members and 24 hours for Premier.

And when you do see us, you'll have the time you need. Our member appointments are longer than standard consultations, giving us space to really address your concerns rather than just ticking boxes.

## Cost Savings

Members enjoy significant savings on services, with discounts ranging from 10% to 20% depending on tier — plus included screenings and check-ins that would cost far more individually.

But the financial benefit of membership goes beyond the discounts. By catching issues early and keeping you healthier overall, proactive care can save you significant money in the long run. Not to mention the value of your time — no more waiting weeks for appointments or sitting in crowded waiting rooms.

## Holistic Support

Health isn't just about test results. Our membership includes access to wellness resources, educational content, and a community of people invested in their wellbeing.

As a member, you'll receive our quarterly newsletter with the latest wellness insights, invitations to exclusive member events, and access to our growing library of guides and resources. You'll be part of a community of people who understand that health is worth investing in.

# How Membership Works

## 1. Choose Your Tier

Select the membership level that fits your needs and health goals. Not sure? Start with Essential and upgrade anytime.

There's no pressure to choose the "right" tier from day one. Many of our members evolve their membership as their needs change. What matters is starting the journey.

## 2. Complete Your Onboarding

Your first appointment includes a comprehensive health assessment and goal-setting session with a practitioner who will take the time to really understand you.

This isn't a rushed medical history — it's a genuine conversation about your health, your concerns, and your goals. We'll review your history, perform appropriate assessments, and begin building your personalised health plan.

## 3. Access Ongoing Care

Use your member benefits throughout the year — from priority booking to exclusive events. Your care team is here when you need them.

As a member, you're never starting from scratch. We know your history, we track your trends, and we're proactive about your care. Questions between appointments? Email us. Concern about a new symptom? We'll see you promptly. Need a referral? We'll coordinate everything.

## 4. Review and Adjust

Annual membership reviews ensure your tier continues to match your evolving needs.

Your health changes over time, and your membership should reflect that. Each year, we'll review your usage, your health status, and your goals to ensure you're on the right plan. Upgrade, downgrade, or stay the same — whatever makes sense for you.

# Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?**

Yes, you can change tiers annually at renewal, or mid-year with 30 days' notice. Upgrades are prorated based on time remaining in your membership year.

**Is there a minimum commitment?**

Memberships are annual with monthly payment options available. Early cancellation fees apply, but we understand that circumstances change — talk to us if you need to make adjustments.

**Do you offer corporate memberships?**

Yes, we offer tailored corporate wellness programmes for organisations that want to invest in their team's health. Benefits include group rates, on-site services, and customised programmes. [Contact us](/contact/) for details.
