# What Sets Us Apart

## Time & Attention

In a world of ten-minute appointments, we take the time to truly understand you.

| Standard Practice | The Cocoon Approach |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| 10-15 minute appointments | 30-90 minute consultations |
| Rushed symptom checks | Comprehensive health conversations |
| One problem per visit | Whole-person assessment |
| Waiting weeks for follow-up | Same-week availability for members |

## Integrated Care Team

Your health doesn't exist in silos — and neither should your care.

| Team Member | Role |
|-------------|------|
| **Physicians & Specialists** | Medical expertise and clinical oversight |
| **Wellness Coaches** | Lifestyle, nutrition, and behaviour change support |
| **Care Coordinators** | Navigation, scheduling, and ongoing communication |
| **Support Staff** | Seamless experience at every touchpoint |

Everyone on your team communicates. Everyone knows your story.

## Advanced Diagnostics

We go beyond the standard panel to uncover what really matters.

| Approach | What It Means |
|----------|---------------|
| **Evidence-Based Testing** | Every test has a clear clinical purpose |
| **Advanced Biomarkers** | Cutting-edge diagnostics when appropriate |
| **Trend Analysis** | We track changes over time, not just snapshots |
| **Integrated Results** | All your data in one place, with clear interpretation |

## Technology-Enabled (Not Technology-Led)

We use technology to enhance care, not replace human connection.

| Technology | How We Use It |
|------------|---------------|
| **Member Portal** | Secure access to your health records |
| **Data Integration** | Your complete health picture in one place |

## Concierge Experience

| Service | What You Experience |

## Evidence-Based Innovation

We stay current with the latest research — but we don't chase trends.

| Our Approach | Why It Matters |

# Our Methodology {#methodology}

## 1. Comprehensive Assessment

Every journey begins with understanding.

| Element | What Happens |
|---------|--------------|
| **Health History** | Detailed review of your medical background |
| **Current Concerns** | Discussion of symptoms, goals, and priorities |
| **Lifestyle Factors** | Understanding sleep, stress, nutrition, movement |
| **Appropriate Diagnostics** | Testing that answers real questions |

## 2. Personalised Protocol Design

One size fits no one.

| Step | Output |
|------|--------|
| **Results Analysis** | Clear interpretation of findings |
| **Goal Setting** | Collaborative definition of health priorities |
| **Action Plan** | Specific, achievable recommendations |
| **Timeline** | Realistic expectations for progress |

## 3. Ongoing Optimisation

Health is a journey, not a destination.

| Activity | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| **Progress Reviews** | Quarterly for members |
| **Plan Adjustments** | As needed based on results and feedback |
| **Support Touchpoints** | Regular check-ins between appointments |
| **Education** | Resources tailored to your needs |

## 4. Results Tracking

What gets measured gets managed.

| Tracking Method | What It Shows |
|-----------------|---------------|
| **Biomarker Trends** | Objective health changes over time |
| **Symptom Monitoring** | Subjective improvements and concerns |
| **Goal Progress** | Movement toward your health objectives |
| **Care Plan Adherence** | What's working and what needs adjustment |
