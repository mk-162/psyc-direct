# Leadership Team

## Sam Naughton, Founder & CEO

**Background:**

Sam founded Cocoon in 2024 after a decade leading Communications at Taylors of Harrogate. Her personal journey through pregnancy loss and the subsequent birth of her son Alby inspired the creation of a healthcare model centred on trust, continuity, and human connection.

**Vision:**

> "While expectant mothers cocoon their baby, we exist to cocoon you and your family every step of the way. That philosophy extends now to every stage of life."
