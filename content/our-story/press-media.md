## Our Story

Cocoon launched in early 2024 with a mission to transform healthcare. Founded by Sam Naughton after a personal journey through pregnancy loss, Cocoon was born from the belief that healthcare could be so much better — more human, more continuous, more proactive.

Since launch, we've supported over 3,000 families and are now expanding into proactive, preventative healthcare for all life stages.
