# Why Cocoon Exists

## The Problem We're Solving

Healthcare systems are under sustained pressure, and people increasingly want proactive, personalised care. But what's available today often falls into two extremes.

On one side, large institutional providers deliver clinical credibility but can feel impersonal and fragmented. Appointments are rushed. Communication is poor. You rarely see the same practitioner twice. And the focus is on treating illness rather than preventing it.

On the other side, direct-to-consumer services offer speed and convenience but lack depth, continuity, and long-term trust. They provide tests and data but little genuine understanding or guidance. You're left to interpret results yourself, without the context of a practitioner who knows your history.

**What's missing is a model that delivers proactive care with clinical integrity, human connection, and continuity over time.**

We built Cocoon to fill that gap. To create a healthcare experience that combines the best of both worlds: clinical excellence with genuine human care, advanced diagnostics with thoughtful interpretation, and cutting-edge science with old-fashioned relationship building.

## Our Answer

Cocoon sits deliberately between those two extremes. Our differentiation is the combination of:

| Element | What It Means |
|---------|---------------|
| **Clinician-Led Care** | Medical expertise at the heart of every decision. Every assessment, every recommendation, every interaction is guided by qualified healthcare professionals who understand the science and the art of medicine. |
| **Designed Experience** | Thoughtful, calm environments that put people at ease. From the moment you walk through our doors, you should feel the difference. No crowded waiting rooms. No harsh lighting. Just a sense of calm and care. |
| **Longitudinal Model** | Relationships that build understanding across life stages. We're not interested in one-off transactions. We want to be your healthcare partner for years to come, building knowledge of your health that compounds over time. |

We're not optimising for one-off tests or single appointments. We're building a relationship-based model where data, insight, and care compound across time. Where your practitioner knows not just your test results, but your story. Where healthcare feels like a partnership, not a transaction.

# Our Mission & Vision

## Mission

To make proactive healthcare feel human, personal, and effortless — through clinical expertise, thoughtful design, and intelligence that works quietly in the background.

This mission guides everything we do. It means that every decision we make, every service we offer, every interaction we have is evaluated against a simple question: does this make healthcare feel more human, more personal, and more effortless for our members?

## Vision

A world where healthcare begins before illness strikes. Where relationships matter more than transactions. Where technology supports clinicians rather than replacing them. Where everyone has access to care that sees them as a whole person, not a collection of symptoms.

We know we're not going to change the entire healthcare system. But we can create a model that proves a different way is possible. That demonstrates that proactive, personalised, human-centred care isn't just a nice idea — it's actually more effective. And that inspires others to raise their standards.

# Our Core Values

Our values aren't just words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every decision we make. When we're faced with a choice, we return to these values to help us find the right path.

## 1. Human First, Always

We design every experience around people, not systems. Technology supports care — it never replaces it.

In an age of increasing automation and AI, it's tempting to believe that technology can solve all our problems. But we've seen what happens when healthcare becomes too automated: patients feel like numbers, practitioners feel like cogs in a machine, and the human connection that makes healing possible is lost.

At Cocoon, we use technology to enhance human care, not replace it. Our systems support our practitioners, freeing them to do what they do best: connect with patients, understand their concerns, and provide genuine care.

> *Decision test: Does this make the experience feel more human or less?*

## 2. Trust Is Our Foundation

We move carefully with health data, technology, and clinical insight. Trust is earned over time and protected deliberately.

Trust is the currency of healthcare. Without it, nothing else matters. Patients won't share the information we need to help them. They won't follow our recommendations. They won't engage with their care.

We earn trust through transparency, consistency, and genuine care. We protect it through rigorous security, ethical practices, and always putting our members' interests first.

> *Decision test: Would we still do this if trust were our only metric?*

## 3. Thoughtful Over Fast

We don't rush to deploy new tools or ideas. We prioritise safety, clarity, and integrity over speed.

In a world that celebrates speed and disruption, we choose thoughtfulness. We take the time to understand new technologies before adopting them. We pilot carefully. We gather feedback. And we only roll out changes when we're confident they'll improve the experience for our members.

This doesn't mean we're slow or resistant to change. It means we're deliberate. We believe that in healthcare, getting it right is more important than getting it fast.

> *Decision test: Is this the right step — or just the next one?*

## 4. Continuity Matters

Health isn't a moment — it's a lifelong journey. We value relationships, context, and long-term understanding over transactions.

Every time you have to explain your medical history to a new practitioner, something is lost. Every time a provider treats you as a blank slate, opportunities for better care are missed. Every time healthcare feels transactional, trust erodes.

We believe in continuity. In practitioners who know your story. In relationships that build over time. In care that sees you as a whole person with a history, not just a set of symptoms in the present moment.

> *Decision test: Does this strengthen the long-term relationship?*

## 5. Quiet Excellence

We believe the best systems work seamlessly in the background. Care should feel calm, effortless, and considered.

There's a difference between impressive and helpful. Between flashy and effective. Between what looks good in a demo and what actually improves people's lives.

We pursue quiet excellence. Systems that just work. Experiences that feel effortless. Care that doesn't draw attention to itself but simply delivers what you need, when you need it.

> *Decision test: Is this helpful — or just impressive?*

# Our Approach to Wellness

## Root-Cause Focus

We don't just treat symptoms — we seek to understand what's really happening beneath the surface.

When you come to us with a concern, we don't just address the obvious issue and send you on your way. We dig deeper. We ask why. We look for the underlying factors that might be contributing to your symptoms.

This root-cause approach takes more time. It requires more investigation. But it leads to more effective, more sustainable solutions. Instead of managing symptoms indefinitely, we aim to address the source of the problem.

## Data-Driven Decisions

Evidence guides everything we do. From the tests we recommend to the treatments we suggest, we rely on the best available research.

We're not interested in fad treatments or unproven approaches. Every recommendation we make is grounded in scientific evidence. We stay current with the latest research, attend conferences, and engage with the medical community to ensure our practices reflect the best available knowledge.

But data alone isn't enough. We also consider your individual circumstances, preferences, and goals. The best care combines evidence with personalisation.

## Whole-Person Care

Your physical health is connected to your mental, emotional, and social wellbeing. We consider the complete picture.

You are not a collection of body systems to be treated in isolation. You're a whole person, and your health reflects that wholeness. Stress affects your digestion. Sleep affects your mood. Relationships affect your immune system. Everything is connected.

Our approach considers all of these factors. We ask about your life, not just your symptoms. We look for patterns and connections. And we recommend interventions that support your overall wellbeing, not just a specific condition.

## Lifestyle Integration

Sustainable health changes happen when they fit into real life. We work with you to create plans that are actually achievable.

The best health plan in the world is useless if you can't stick to it. We understand that you have a life — work, family, responsibilities, preferences. We don't expect you to completely overhaul your existence to follow our recommendations.

Instead, we work with you to find approaches that fit your life. Small changes that add up over time. Strategies that feel sustainable. Support that helps you navigate the inevitable challenges.

# The Story So Far

## Our Beginning

Cocoon launched in early 2024, founded by Sam Naughton after a personal journey through pregnancy loss and the realisation that healthcare could be so much better.

Sam's experience trying to navigate the healthcare system during a vulnerable time revealed gaps that many people face: rushed appointments, fragmented care, lack of continuity, and a focus on treating illness rather than supporting wellness. She knew there had to be a better way.

## Early Success

Since launch, we've supported over 3,000 families through fertility, pregnancy, and early life care — building trust in some of the most sensitive healthcare moments.

This early focus on maternity services taught us invaluable lessons about what matters most in healthcare: trust, continuity, communication, and genuine care. It shaped our approach to clinical governance, our standards for patient experience, and our understanding of what people need from their healthcare providers.

## Natural Evolution

Our expansion into preventative health is a natural evolution of that foundation. The same principles that guided our maternity services — trust, continuity, human connection — now inform our approach to lifelong wellness.

We're taking what we learned from supporting families through some of life's most important moments and applying it to proactive healthcare for all life stages. Because everyone deserves care that sees them as a whole person, not just a collection of symptoms.

## This Is Cocoon V2

Built with experience, intention, and care.

This next chapter of Cocoon represents everything we've learned and everything we believe about what healthcare can be. It's not just an expansion of services — it's a statement of intent. A commitment to proving that a different kind of healthcare is possible. And an invitation to join us on the journey.
