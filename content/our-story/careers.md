# Why Work at Cocoon?

## Our Mission

To make proactive healthcare feel human, personal, and effortless. Every role at Cocoon contributes to this mission.

## Our Culture

| Value | How We Live It |
|-------|----------------|
| **Human First** | We care for our team as we care for our members |
| **Trust** | Autonomy, transparency, and accountability |
| **Thoughtful** | We take time to do things right |
| **Continuity** | We invest in long-term relationships with our team |
| **Quiet Excellence** | Recognition for work that makes a difference |

## What We Offer

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Competitive Salary** | Market-rate compensation |
| **Health & Wellness** | Cocoon membership for you and family |
| **Professional Development** | Training and education budget |
| **Flexible Working** | Where the role allows |
| **Pension** | Employer contribution |
| **Annual Leave** | Generous allowance |
| **Team Events** | Regular social and wellness activities |

# Who We're Looking For

## Clinical Roles

| Role | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| **Physicians** | GMC registration, relevant specialisation |
| **Nurses** | NMC registration, relevant experience |
| **Allied Health** | Appropriate registration and qualifications |

## Wellness Roles

| Role | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| **Nutritionists** | Registered nutritionist or dietitian |
| **Fitness Specialists** | Relevant certifications, experience |
| **Mental Health Practitioners** | Appropriate qualifications and registration |

## Operations Roles

| Role | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| **Care Coordinators** | Healthcare admin experience, excellent communication |
| **Reception/Front of House** | Customer service experience, warm demeanour |
| **Operations** | Relevant experience in healthcare or hospitality |
