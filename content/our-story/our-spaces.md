# How to Find Us

## Location

Cocoon is based in the heart of Harrogate, North Yorkshire — a five-minute stroll from Harrogate Train Station, Bus Station, and the High Street, and just a stone's throw away from Waitrose.

## Directions

You'll find the clinic off the roundabout on Victoria Avenue, down a short private driveway between Kip McGrath and the Methodist church. Tapping our postcode (HG1 5RD) into your maps app will bring you directly here.

## By Train

| Station | Walking Time |
|---------|--------------|
| Harrogate Station | 5 minutes |

## By Car

### Parking

Please don't pull down the narrow driveway to the clinic; there's very limited space and we'd hate you to get stuck or damage your car.

| Parking Option | Details |
|----------------|---------|
| **Victoria Avenue** | Plenty of parking right outside the clinic |
| **Waitrose Car Park** | Two hours free with purchase (1-minute walk) |

Our clinic is designed around one principle: you should feel at ease the moment you arrive.

| Element | Our Approach |
|---------|--------------|
| **Lighting** | Natural and warm, never harsh |
| **Colours** | Earth tones that promote relaxation |
| **Furniture** | Comfortable, residential feel |
