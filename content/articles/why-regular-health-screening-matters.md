## The Case for Proactive Health

We service our cars every year. We maintain our homes. Yet most of us wait until something goes wrong before we think about our health. By that point, we're dealing with symptoms — not preventing them.

Regular health screening is the single most effective way to catch serious conditions early, when they're most treatable and outcomes are best.

## What We're Looking For

A comprehensive health screening isn't just a blood test. At Cocoon, our screenings examine:

- **Cardiovascular health** — cholesterol, blood pressure, arterial stiffness, heart rhythm
- **Metabolic function** — blood glucose, HbA1c, insulin resistance markers
- **Hormonal balance** — thyroid, testosterone, cortisol, vitamin D
- **Organ function** — liver, kidney, full blood count
- **Cancer markers** — PSA, CA-125, and targeted panels based on your risk profile

## The Numbers Speak

Research consistently shows that early detection dramatically improves outcomes:

- **Heart disease** detected early has a 90%+ survival rate
- **Type 2 diabetes** caught at pre-diabetic stage can often be reversed entirely
- **Most cancers** have significantly better prognosis when found before symptoms appear

## When to Start

We recommend baseline screening from age 30, with annual reviews from 40. If you have a family history of heart disease, cancer, or diabetes, earlier screening is advisable.

## The Cocoon Approach

Our screenings are designed around *you*, not a generic checklist. Every screening begins with a detailed health history and lifestyle assessment, so the clinical team can focus on what matters most for your individual risk profile.

Results are reviewed personally by a clinician — not posted through your letterbox. We sit down with you, explain what everything means, and build a clear plan for the year ahead.
