# Portal Overview

My Cocoon is your secure gateway to everything related to your health journey with us. Designed for simplicity and privacy, it puts your health information at your fingertips.

| Feature | What You Can Do |
|---------|-----------------|
| **View Results** | Access all your test results and health records |
| **Book Appointments** | Schedule visits with priority member access |
| **Message Your Team** | Secure communication with your care providers |
| **Track Progress** | See trends in your health metrics over time |
| **Manage Account** | Update details, payment methods, and preferences |

# Security & Privacy

Your health data is protected by the highest security standards.

| Security Feature | Implementation |
|------------------|----------------|
| **Encryption** | All data encrypted in transit and at rest |
| **Access Control** | Multi-factor authentication required |
| **Audit Logs** | All access tracked and monitored |
| **GDPR Compliant** | Full compliance with data protection regulations |
| **Regular Security Reviews** | Ongoing assessment and improvement |

**Your Data, Your Control:**
- You control who can access your records
- Request data export at any time
- Opt out of data sharing (where not required by law)
- Delete your account and data (subject to medical record retention requirements)
