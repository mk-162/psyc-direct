# Getting Started

## How do I book my first appointment?

You can book online for most services through our website, or call us on 01423 567437 during clinic hours. If you're unsure which service is right for you, we're happy to discuss your needs and recommend the best option.

## What should I expect at my first appointment?

Your first appointment typically includes:
- A comprehensive consultation to understand your health history and concerns
- Any necessary examinations or diagnostic tests
- Discussion of initial findings and next steps
- Clear timeline for results and follow-up

## Do I need to prepare for my appointment?

Some tests require fasting or other preparation. We'll send you detailed instructions before your appointment. Generally, bring a list of current medications and any questions you want to ask.

## What's included in membership?

| Tier | Key Benefits |

| **Complete** | Everything in Essential + quarterly check-ins, nutrition consult, 15% discount |

[Compare Memberships](/membership/)

## Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can change tiers at your annual renewal, or mid-year with 30 days' notice. Upgrades are prorated based on time remaining.

## Is there a minimum commitment?

Memberships are annual with monthly payment options available. Early cancellation fees apply.

# Billing & Insurance

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards. Bank transfers can be arranged for larger amounts with prior agreement.

## Do you accept health insurance?

We don't currently bill insurance directly, but we can provide detailed receipts for you to claim reimbursement. Check with your insurer about coverage for private healthcare.

## Can I get a refund?

Refunds are provided according to our cancellation policy:
- More than 48 hours notice: Full refund
- 24-48 hours notice: 50% refund
- Less than 24 hours: No refund

# General Wellness

## How often should I have a health screening?

We recommend annual comprehensive screening for most adults. Those with specific risk factors may benefit from more frequent monitoring. We'll personalise recommendations based on your profile.

## Do you replace my NHS GP?

No — we complement NHS services, not replace them. We recommend maintaining your NHS GP registration and attending routine NHS appointments.

## Can I see the same practitioner each time?

Yes, we prioritise continuity of care. Members can request specific practitioners, and we'll do our best to accommodate.
