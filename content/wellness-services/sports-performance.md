# Performance Through Understanding

Whether you're a competitive athlete or serious about your fitness, understanding your body's unique physiology is the key to unlocking your potential. Our Sports Performance service goes beyond standard health checks to assess the specific biomarkers and physiological factors that affect athletic performance.

| Standard Health Check | Sports Performance Assessment |

# Your Questions Answered

**How often should athletes be tested?**

We recommend baseline testing at the start of a training block, with follow-ups every 3-4 months or around key competitions.

**Can you work with my coach?**

Absolutely. With your permission, we can share results and recommendations to integrate with your training programme.

**Do you test for doping?**

No — our testing is for health and performance optimisation only. We do not provide anti-doping testing services.
