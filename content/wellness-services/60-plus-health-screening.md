# Ageing Well, Not Just Ageing

Your sixties and beyond can be among the most fulfilling decades of life. It's a time when many people find new freedoms, pursue long-deferred passions, and enjoy the fruits of their labour. But these years also bring new health considerations that deserve attention.

Our 60+ Health Screening is designed to address the specific health priorities of this life stage: maintaining cognitive function, protecting cardiovascular health, preserving bone strength, and optimising metabolic wellness. It's about ageing well — not just living longer, but living better.

We believe in proactive, not reactive, healthcare. Catching potential issues early gives you the best chance to address them effectively and maintain the quality of life you deserve. Many of the conditions that become more common after 60 — heart disease, cognitive decline, osteoporosis, diabetes — develop gradually and can be detected long before symptoms appear.

The good news is that there's so much you can do to protect your health at this stage of life. Exercise, nutrition, social connection, and mental stimulation all have profound effects on how we age. Our screening provides the information you need to make informed choices about your health, and our practitioners provide the guidance to help you implement them.

# What's Included

## Core Assessment

Our core assessment covers the essential markers of health for adults 60 and over, with particular attention to the conditions that become more prevalent at this life stage.

| Test Category | What We Check |
|---------------|---------------|
| **Cardiovascular Health** | Comprehensive lipid profile including advanced markers, blood pressure monitoring, ECG to assess heart rhythm and detect any abnormalities |
| **Cognitive Screening** | Memory assessment, cognitive function tests to establish baseline and detect any early changes |
| **Metabolic Function** | Blood glucose, HbA1c, insulin markers — diabetes risk increases with age but can be managed with early detection |
| **Bone Health** | Vitamin D, calcium, bone turnover markers — essential for preventing osteoporosis |
| **Thyroid Function** | Full thyroid panel including antibodies — thyroid issues are common and often undiagnosed in older adults |
| **Nutritional Status** | B12, folate, iron panel, comprehensive vitamin assessment — nutritional needs change as we age |
| **Kidney & Liver Function** | Comprehensive metabolic panel — these vital organs need monitoring as we get older |
| **Inflammatory Markers** | hs-CRP for cardiovascular and general health — chronic inflammation underlies many age-related conditions |

These markers tell a comprehensive story about your current health status. But we don't just hand you a report — we sit down with you to explain what everything means and discuss what steps you might consider to optimise your health.

You'll receive a comprehensive health questionnaire covering your medical history, current medications, and any concerns. We'll also request any recent test results from your GP if you have them — we don't want to duplicate tests unnecessarily.

## During Your Appointment (90-120 minutes)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|

# Your Questions Answered

**Will this screening detect dementia?**

Our cognitive screening can identify changes in cognitive function that warrant further investigation. Early detection allows for timely referral to specialist services and potential interventions. Many causes of cognitive decline are treatable, which is why early assessment matters.

**Should I bring my medications to the appointment?**

Yes, please bring all medications and supplements you currently take, including dosages. This is essential for our medication review. If you have a list from your GP or pharmacy, that works too.

**How does this differ from my NHS health check?**

NHS health checks are valuable but time-limited. Our screening offers extended consultation time, more comprehensive testing, and detailed follow-up with personalised recommendations. We complement rather than replace NHS services.

**Will you coordinate with my GP?**

Absolutely. With your permission, we'll send a summary of your results to your NHS GP. We believe in coordination, not duplication, and want to ensure all your healthcare providers have the information they need.
