# When Tired Isn't Just Tired

Feeling tired occasionally is part of life. But when fatigue persists despite rest, affects your daily functioning, or comes with other symptoms, it's worth investigating. Our "Tired All The Time" assessment is designed to uncover the underlying causes of persistent low energy.

| Common Causes | What We Assess |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Thyroid Dysfunction** | Full thyroid panel including antibodies |
| **Nutritional Deficiencies** | Iron, B12, vitamin D, and more |
| **Blood Sugar Dysregulation** | Glucose, insulin, HbA1c |
| **Adrenal/HPA Axis** | Cortisol patterns, stress response |
| **Sleep Disorders** | Sleep quality assessment |
| **Chronic Inflammation** | Inflammatory markers |

# Your Questions Answered

**How long before I feel better?**

This depends on the cause. Some deficiencies respond within weeks, while others may take months. We'll set realistic expectations based on your findings.

**Will you refer me to a specialist if needed?**

Yes — if we identify conditions requiring specialist care, we'll facilitate appropriate referrals.

**Can fatigue be psychological?**

Sometimes fatigue has psychological components. We assess holistically and can refer for psychological support if indicated.
