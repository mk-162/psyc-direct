# A Different Approach to Weight

Weight is complex. It's not simply about calories in, calories out. Hormones, metabolism, sleep, stress, and genetics all play a role. Our Weight Clinic takes a comprehensive, evidence-based approach to understanding and addressing your unique situation.

| Common Approach | Our Approach |
|-----------------|--------------|
| One-size-fits-all diet | Personalised nutrition based on your metabolism |
| Quick fixes | Sustainable lifestyle changes |
| Calorie counting alone | Understanding hormonal and metabolic factors |
| Shame-based motivation | Supportive, non-judgmental care |

|------------|------------------|

# Your Questions Answered

**Do you prescribe weight loss medication?**

We focus on lifestyle and metabolic optimisation first. In some cases, we may refer to medical colleagues for medication evaluation.

**How quickly will I see results?**

Sustainable weight loss is typically 0.5-1kg per week. We prioritise long-term success over rapid results.

**Do I need to follow a specific diet?**

We personalise recommendations to your preferences, culture, and lifestyle. No rigid meal plans unless you prefer them.
