# Your Questions Answered

**Can cognitive decline be prevented?**

While not all cognitive decline is preventable, research shows that lifestyle factors significantly influence brain health. Early intervention matters.

**Will this diagnose dementia?**

Our assessment can identify cognitive changes that warrant further investigation. Diagnosis of dementia requires specialist evaluation.

**How often should I be tested?**

We recommend baseline testing in your 50s, with follow-up every 1-2 years, or more frequently if concerns arise.
