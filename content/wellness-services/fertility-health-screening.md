# Understanding Your Fertility

Whether you're just starting to think about having a baby or have been trying for a while, understanding your reproductive health is empowering. Our Fertility Health Screening provides a comprehensive assessment of the factors that influence fertility, helping you make informed decisions about your family planning.

| Screening For | What We Assess |
|---------------|----------------|
| **Hormonal Health** | Key hormones regulating ovulation and sperm production |
| **Ovarian Reserve** | Egg quantity and quality indicators |
| **Sperm Health** | Comprehensive semen analysis |
| **General Health** | Factors affecting fertility |
| **Nutritional Status** | Vitamins and minerals essential for reproduction |

# Your Questions Answered

**When should we have fertility screening?**

Ideally before you start trying to conceive, or if you've been trying for 6-12 months without success.

**Does screening guarantee we'll conceive?**

No — fertility is complex. Screening helps identify factors that may affect fertility and guides next steps.

**What if we need IVF?**

We work closely with fertility clinics and can facilitate referrals to appropriate specialists.
