# Your Questions Answered

**What's the difference between Monthly Membership and Premier?**

Monthly Membership provides regular check-ins and blood work. Premier adds biannual comprehensive screenings, a dedicated care coordinator, and higher service discounts.

**Can I cancel anytime?**

Monthly Membership has a 3-month minimum commitment, then monthly rolling with 30 days' notice.

**What if I miss a monthly check-in?**

We understand schedules change. Missed check-ins can be rescheduled or credited toward other services.
