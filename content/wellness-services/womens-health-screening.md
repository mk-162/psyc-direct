# Understanding Your Health

Women's health needs evolve throughout life — from reproductive years through perimenopause and beyond. Our bodies go through remarkable changes, and understanding what's happening beneath the surface can be transformative for how we feel and function.

Our Women's Health Screening is designed to address the specific health concerns women face, providing clarity and actionable insights tailored to your life stage. We look at the full picture: hormonal balance, nutritional status, metabolic function, and the interconnected systems that affect how you feel every day.

Whether you're experiencing symptoms you can't explain, planning for the future, or simply want to understand your body better, this screening gives you the knowledge to make informed decisions about your health.

## Why Women's Health Needs a Different Approach

Women's bodies are wonderfully complex, with hormonal fluctuations that affect everything from energy and mood to metabolism and sleep. Yet too often, women's health concerns are dismissed or oversimplified. Symptoms are attributed to "stress" or "just getting older" without proper investigation.

At Cocoon, we take women's health seriously. We understand that fatigue might be related to iron deficiency or thyroid dysfunction. That irregular cycles might indicate hormonal imbalances that are treatable. That mood changes during perimenopause are real and deserve support. And that every woman deserves care that's tailored to her unique physiology and life stage.

# What's Included

## Core Assessment

Our core assessment covers the essential markers of women's health, providing insights into the systems that most affect how you feel and function.

| Test Category | What We Check |
|---------------|---------------|
| **Hormonal Balance** | Oestrogen, progesterone, testosterone, FSH, LH — the key hormones that regulate your cycle, mood, energy, and overall wellbeing |
| **Thyroid Function** | TSH, T3, T4, thyroid antibodies — thyroid issues are far more common in women and can cause a wide range of symptoms |
| **Metabolic Health** | Blood glucose, HbA1c, lipid profile — metabolic function affects energy, weight, and long-term health |
| **Nutritional Status** | Vitamin D, B12, folate, ferritin, iron panel — nutritional deficiencies are common and can cause significant symptoms |
| **Liver & Kidney Function** | Comprehensive metabolic panel — these vital organs affect everything from energy to hormone processing |
| **Cardiovascular Markers** | Cholesterol profile, blood pressure — heart disease is the leading cause of death in women, and risk factors can be detected early |

These markers tell a story about your current health status. But we don't just hand you a report with numbers — we sit down with you to explain what everything means in the context of your symptoms, your history, and your goals.

# Your Questions Answered

**Should I book this or the Menopause Specialist service?**

If you're primarily experiencing menopausal symptoms and want focused support, the Menopause Specialist service may be more appropriate. This screening is ideal for a broader health assessment or if you're unsure what's causing your symptoms. If you're uncertain, contact us and we'll help you choose.

**Can I have this screening while on hormonal contraception?**

Yes, though some hormone levels will be affected. We'll interpret your results accordingly and may recommend timing tests around your pill-free week if appropriate. Let us know what contraception you're using when booking.

**Is this a replacement for my NHS smear test?**

No — we strongly recommend keeping up with routine NHS screenings. Our cervical health add-on can complement but does not replace your regular smear test. Prevention is about doing both.
