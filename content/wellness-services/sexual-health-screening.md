# Your Sexual Health Matters

Sexual health is an important part of overall wellbeing, yet many people avoid screening due to embarrassment or inconvenience. At Cocoon, we provide confidential, comprehensive sexual health services in a supportive environment where you can ask questions and get the care you need without judgment.

# Your Questions Answered

**How confidential is the service?**

Completely. Your results are not shared with anyone without your consent. Not your GP, not your employer, not your partner.

**What if I test positive?**

We provide treatment or referral as needed, partner notification support, and follow-up care. Many STIs are easily treatable.

**How often should I be tested?**

This depends on your risk factors. Generally, annually for sexually active adults, or more frequently with new partners or higher risk.
