# How It Works

## 1. Book Your Consultation

Schedule online or call us. We'll match you with the right practitioner for your needs and send you any preparation instructions in advance.

Booking should be easy, and we've designed our system to make it so. You can book directly through our website for most services, or call us if you prefer to speak to someone. If you're not sure which service is right for you, we're happy to discuss your needs and make a recommendation.

## 2. Comprehensive Assessment

Your appointment includes thorough consultation, appropriate diagnostics, and time to discuss your concerns. No rushing. No ticking boxes.

This is where the Cocoon difference really shows. Our consultations are unhurried, typically lasting 30-90 minutes depending on the service. We take the time to understand your history, your concerns, and your goals. We explain what we're doing and why. And we make sure you have the opportunity to ask questions.

## 3. Personalised Follow-Up

Receive clear results, actionable recommendations, and ongoing support to achieve your health goals.

Results are typically available within 5-10 working days, depending on the tests performed. You'll receive a comprehensive report, and we'll schedule a follow-up consultation to discuss everything in detail. This is where we explain what your results mean, answer your questions, and discuss next steps.
