## What is it?
The EHCP & Statutory Assessment service is designed to support schools, local authorities, and multi-academy trusts with responsive, evidence-based interventions. Step-by-step guides to EHCP processes, timelines, and statutory obligations. Our Educational Psychologists deliver insights that go beyond standard assessments, providing practical, classroom-ready strategies that staff can implement immediately.
Educational settings often face long waiting lists and complex SEND requirements. We bypass the backlog by matching your setting with an available, HCPC-registered Educational Psychologist usually within 48 hours. Our reports are fully compliant with statutory guidelines and are quality-checked to ensure all recommendations are clear, actionable, and tailored to the student's specific learning environment.
