## What is it?
The School Consultations service is designed to support schools, local authorities, and multi-academy trusts with responsive, evidence-based interventions. Direct support for complex cases. Our EP meets with staff, observes in classroom, and helps you develop workable strategies. Our Educational Psychologists deliver insights that go beyond standard assessments, providing practical, classroom-ready strategies that staff can implement immediately.
Educational settings often face long waiting lists and complex SEND requirements. We bypass the backlog by matching your setting with an available, HCPC-registered Educational Psychologist usually within 48 hours. Our reports are fully compliant with statutory guidelines and are quality-checked to ensure all recommendations are clear, actionable, and tailored to the student's specific learning environment.

## How it works
Step 1: The Brief. Contact our team to outline your specific requirements, deadlines, and the details of the case or assessment needed.
Step 2: Fast Matching. Within 24 to 48 hours, your Client Manager will provide CVs and transparent fee quotes from vetted experts suited to your needs.
Step 3: Assessment. The selected psychologist or psychiatrist carries out a thorough evaluation, utilizing standardized methodologies and clinical interviews.
Step 4: Delivery. You receive a fully proofread, quality-assured report delivered securely and on time, ready for your next steps.
