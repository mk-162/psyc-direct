## What is it?
The Practice Management is designed to empower our clients and professionals with the knowledge and tools they need. CV guides, business planning, fee-setting, and growing your independent practice. Navigating the landscape of expert instruction, SEND tribunals, or clinical compliance can be complex, and having the right resources on hand is invaluable.
We have distilled 15+ years of industry experience into practical, accessible guidance. By utilizing these resources, solicitors, SENCOs, and independent experts can ensure they are following best practices, meeting statutory requirements, and making informed decisions at every stage of the process.
