## What is it?
Steady Pipeline of Cases is a core part of how we support our network of independent professionals. Continuous stream of expert witness and educational assessments from 1,000+ clients. No feast-or-famine months. Cases matched to your specialism and availability. We believe that when our psychologists and psychiatrists are supported, fairly compensated, and relieved of administrative burdens, they deliver their best clinical work.
Joining Psychology Direct means becoming part of a community. We handle the logistics—from initial enquiry and fee negotiation to appointment scheduling and invoicing—so that you can focus entirely on your assessments. With a steady pipeline of diverse cases, you have the flexibility to accept the work that aligns with your specialisms and availability.

## How it works
Step 1: Application. Submit your CV and HCPC/GMC registration details to our network team.
Step 2: Vetting and Onboarding. We review your credentials, sample reports, and DBS status to ensure our quality standards are met.
Step 3: Case Matching. Your dedicated manager will contact you with cases that match your specific expertise, location, and stated availability.
Step 4: Clinical Focus. You conduct the assessment and write the report; we handle the invoicing, client liaison, and administrative follow-up.
