## What is it?
Our CPD Webinars forms part of our commitment to ongoing professional development and community building. Interactive online sessions on topical issues relevant to psychologists and psychiatrists. Experts lead discussions. Earn CPD hours. Free for our professionals. Staying current with clinical practices, legal guidelines, and educational strategies is essential for any practicing psychologist or psychiatrist.
These sessions are designed by experts, for experts. Whether it is a deep dive into complex trauma, navigating the latest MoJ updates, or understanding new neurodevelopmental criteria, our events provide high-quality, CPD-accredited learning in an interactive and supportive environment.

## How it works
Step 1: Explore. Browse the available materials, tools, or event schedules provided on our platform.
Step 2: Access and Register. Download the resources directly or register your place for upcoming live events and webinars.
Step 3: Engage. Utilize the calculators, read the guidance, or participate in the live Q and A sessions to enhance your practice.
Step 4: Ongoing Support. Reach out to our team if you need further bespoke advice, case consultations, or extended training.
