## What is it?
Our Risk of Re-offending service provides rigorous, court-ready assessments for complex legal proceedings. Validated risk assessment using structured tools and professional judgment. Conducted by HCPC and GMC registered psychologists and psychiatrists, our reports are robust, independent, and strictly adhere to MoJ Practice Directions.
We understand that in legal contexts, timelines are critical and the quality of evidence can determine the outcome of a case. By instructing an expert through Psychology Direct, you gain access to a network of over 1,000 vetted professionals, ensuring that your case is matched with an expert who possesses the precise clinical experience required. Every report goes through our stringent quality assurance process before it reaches your desk.

## How it works
Step 1: The Brief. Contact our team to outline your specific requirements, deadlines, and the details of the case or assessment needed.
Step 2: Fast Matching. Within 24 to 48 hours, your Client Manager will provide CVs and transparent fee quotes from vetted experts suited to your needs.
Step 3: Assessment. The selected psychologist or psychiatrist carries out a thorough evaluation, utilizing standardized methodologies and clinical interviews.
Step 4: Delivery. You receive a fully proofread, quality-assured report delivered securely and on time, ready for your next steps.
