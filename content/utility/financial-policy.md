## Payment Terms

### Membership Fees
- Membership fees are charged monthly or annually, depending on your selected billing cycle
- Annual memberships receive a discount equivalent to two months free
- Payment is due on the first day of each billing period
- We accept payment by direct debit, credit/debit card, or bank transfer

### Service Fees
- Individual services not covered by membership are payable at the time of service
- Quotes for comprehensive health screenings are provided prior to booking
- Additional tests or services requested during your visit will be quoted and require approval before proceeding

### Late Payments
- Membership fees not received within 7 days of the due date may incur a late payment fee of £25
- Continued non-payment may result in suspension of membership benefits
- We reserve the right to refer outstanding balances to a collection agency

## Insurance

### Private Health Insurance
- Cocoon Wellness works with most major private health insurers
- Coverage varies by insurer and policy—please verify your benefits before services
- We can provide detailed invoices and clinical letters to support insurance claims
- You are responsible for any amounts not covered by your insurance

### Self-Pay Options
- Members without insurance coverage are welcome and receive the same high-quality care
- Self-pay members may be eligible for payment plans for services over £500
- We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees

### Insurance Verification
- Please provide your insurance details at least 48 hours before your appointment
- We will verify your benefits and inform you of any estimated out-of-pocket costs
- You are responsible for understanding your policy's coverage, deductibles, and co-payments

## Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

### Appointments
We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while respecting the time of our care team:

- **More than 48 hours' notice**: No charge, full refund of any pre-payment
- **24–48 hours' notice**: 50% cancellation fee applies
- **Less than 24 hours' notice**: Full service fee charged
- **No-show without notice**: Full service fee charged

### Membership Cancellation
- Monthly memberships: Cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice
- Annual memberships: No refunds for early cancellation except in exceptional circumstances
- Upon cancellation, you may access services at non-member rates for 30 days

### How to Cancel or Reschedule
- **Online**: Through the [My Cocoon](/my-cocoon/) portal *(Coming soon)*
- **Phone**: 01423 567437 during business hours
- **Email**: appointments@cocoonwellness.com (must be received during business hours to count as same-day)

## Refund Policy

### Service Refunds
- If you are dissatisfied with a service, please contact us within 14 days
- Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis
- Diagnostic tests that have been processed cannot be refunded
- Completed consultations are generally non-refundable

### Membership Refunds
- Monthly fees already charged are not refundable
- Annual memberships: Prorated refunds considered within the first 30 days only
- Refunds due to medical incapacity are reviewed individually with appropriate documentation

## Billing Procedures

### Invoices & Receipts
- Detailed invoices are provided for all services
- Receipts are available upon request for any payment
- Monthly statements are sent to annual members showing all activity

### Disputed Charges
If you believe there is an error on your bill:
1. Contact our billing team at **billing@cocoonwellness.com** within 14 days
2. Provide specific details about the disputed charge
3. We will investigate and respond within 10 business days
4. You are not required to pay disputed amounts during the investigation

### Payment Plans
For services exceeding £1,000, we offer interest-free payment plans:
- 3-month plan: Equal monthly payments
- 6-month plan: Available for services over £2,000
- Direct debit required for payment plan arrangements

## Financial Hardship

We believe that financial circumstances should not prevent access to quality care. If you are experiencing financial hardship:

- Contact our Member Experience Director to discuss your situation
- We may offer extended payment plans on a case-by-case basis
- Discounted services may be available in exceptional circumstances
- All discussions are treated with strict confidentiality
