## How to Book

We offer several convenient ways to schedule your visit:

### Online Booking
Our online booking system allows you to select your preferred service, choose a date and time that works for you, and receive instant confirmation. Available 24/7 for your convenience.

### By Phone
Prefer to speak with someone? Call us on **01423 567437** during business hours (Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm; Saturday, 9am–1pm) and our care coordination team will be happy to assist you.

### Email
Send your booking request to **hello@cocoonwellness.com** and we'll respond within one business day with available options.

### Member Portal
Existing members can book directly through the [My Cocoon](/my-cocoon/) portal for priority access to appointments.

## What to Expect

### Before Your Visit
- You'll receive a confirmation email with your appointment details
- For health screenings, we'll send preparation instructions specific to your tests
- Complete any required forms through our secure patient portal
- Note any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss with your practitioner

### When You Arrive
- Allow a few extra minutes for your first visit to complete any remaining paperwork
- Our reception team will welcome you and guide you through the check-in process
- Enjoy complimentary refreshments in our wellness lounge
- We'll ensure you're comfortable before your consultation begins

### Your Consultation
- Your practitioner will take time to understand your health history, concerns, and goals
- Depending on your service, diagnostic assessments may be performed
- You'll receive clear explanations and personalised recommendations
- Together, we'll develop a plan tailored to your needs

## Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. This allows us to offer your slot to another member. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.
