# What is the CQC?

The Care Quality Commission (CQC) is the independent regulator of all health and social care services in England. Their role is to:

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Register** | Ensure providers meet essential standards |
| **Monitor** | Regular inspections and ongoing oversight |
| **Inspect** | Comprehensive assessments of care quality |
| **Rate** | Publish ratings to inform the public |
| **Enforce** | Take action when standards aren't met |
