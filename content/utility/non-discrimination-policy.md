## Our Commitment

At Cocoon Wellness, we believe that:
- Every person deserves respectful, high-quality healthcare
- Diversity enriches our community and strengthens our care
- Discrimination has no place in healthcare
- Accessibility is a fundamental right, not a privilege
- Cultural competence is essential to effective care

---

## Protected Characteristics

We do not discriminate on the basis of:

### Protected Characteristics (Equality Act 2010)
- **Age** – We welcome members of all ages and adapt our care accordingly
- **Disability** – We make reasonable adjustments to ensure accessible care
- **Gender Reassignment** – We respect and affirm all gender identities
- **Marriage & Civil Partnership** – All relationship statuses are treated equally
- **Pregnancy & Maternity** – We provide supportive care throughout all life stages
- **Race** – Including colour, nationality, and ethnic or national origins
- **Religion or Belief** – Including philosophical beliefs and lack of belief
- **Sex** – Both biological and gender-based considerations
- **Sexual Orientation** – All orientations are welcome and respected

### Additional Protected Categories
- Socioeconomic status
- Immigration status
- Genetic information
- Health status (HIV status, other conditions)
- Veteran or military status
- Body size or weight

## In Our Services

### Equal Access to Care
- All eligible individuals may apply for membership regardless of protected characteristics
- Clinical decisions are based solely on medical necessity and evidence
- Wait times and appointment availability are not influenced by personal characteristics
- We offer flexible appointment times to accommodate religious observances and work schedules

### Reasonable Adjustments
We are committed to making reasonable adjustments for members with disabilities:
- Step-free access to all clinical areas
- Accessible consultation rooms and facilities
- Assistance for members with visual or hearing impairments
- Extended appointment times when needed
- Support for members with learning disabilities or cognitive conditions
- Flexibility for members with mental health conditions

### Language & Communication
- Interpreting services available for members with limited English proficiency
- Written materials available in alternative formats (large print, Braille, easy read)
- Culturally appropriate care that respects dietary requirements, modesty needs, and cultural practices

## In Our Workplace

### Employment Practices
Cocoon Wellness is an equal opportunity employer:
- Recruitment and hiring based on merit and qualifications
- Equal pay for equal work
- Professional development opportunities available to all staff
- Zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or discrimination
- Flexible working arrangements considered for all roles

### Staff Training
All team members receive:
- Equality and diversity training as part of induction
- Regular updates on inclusive practices
- Cultural competence training specific to healthcare
- Training on recognising and challenging unconscious bias

## Reporting Discrimination

### If You Experience Discrimination
If you believe you have been treated unfairly by Cocoon Wellness:

1. **Immediate Concerns**: Speak with the staff member involved or their supervisor
2. **Formal Complaint**: Submit a written complaint to our Member Experience Director
3. **External Reporting**: Contact the Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) or Care Quality Commission (CQC)

We take all complaints seriously and will:
- Acknowledge your complaint within 2 working days
- Conduct a thorough, impartial investigation
- Provide a full response within 20 working days
- Take appropriate action if discrimination is confirmed

### Anonymous Reporting
If you prefer to report anonymously:
- Use our confidential feedback form *(Coming soon)*
- Email concerns@codiscrimination@cocoonwellness.com from an anonymous address

## Accessibility Statement

Cocoon Wellness is committed to digital and physical accessibility:

### Physical Accessibility
- Level access to all areas
- Accessible parking spaces
- Hearing loop systems
- Accessible toilet facilities
- Quiet spaces for members with sensory sensitivities

### Digital Accessibility
Our website aims to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards:
- Compatible with screen readers
- Keyboard navigable
- Resizable text
- Colour contrast compliant
- Alternative text for images

For detailed accessibility information, visit our [Accessibility Statement](/accessibility-statement/).
