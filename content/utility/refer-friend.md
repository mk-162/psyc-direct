## How It Works

### 1. Share Your Experience
Tell friends, family, or colleagues about your journey with Cocoon. Share what you love most—whether it's the attentive care, the comprehensive assessments, or simply feeling truly heard for the first time.

### 2. They Book a Visit
When your referral books their first appointment, they'll mention your name or use your unique referral code. They'll receive a warm welcome and the same exceptional experience that made you a member.

### 3. You Both Benefit
Once your referral completes their first paid service, you'll both receive rewards as a thank you from us.

---

## Referral Rewards

### For You (The Referrer)
- **£50 credit** toward your next service or membership renewal
- Priority access to new offerings and events
- Entry into our quarterly prize draw for exclusive wellness experiences

### For Your Friend (The Referral)
- **£50 off** their first health screening or wellness service
- Waived new member consultation fee (value: £95)
- Complimentary wellness assessment

*Rewards apply to referrals who are not existing members and who complete their first paid service. Credits cannot be combined with other offers.*

## Ways to Refer

### Online Referral
Share your unique referral link via email, text, or social media. Your friends can book directly through your link, ensuring you both receive your rewards.

### In-Person
Pick up referral cards during your next visit. These elegant cards can be handed directly to friends and include your unique referral code.

### Email Introduction
Want to make a personal introduction? Send us an email at **referrals@cocoonwellness.com** with your friend's details, and we'll reach out with a special invitation on your behalf.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
Not at all. Refer as many friends as you'd like. There's no cap on the rewards you can earn.

**When will I receive my referral credit?**
Credits are applied to your account within 7 days of your referral completing their first paid service.

**Can I refer someone who's been to Cocoon before?**
This programme is designed for new members only. If someone has previously visited Cocoon, they won't be eligible for referral rewards.

**Do my credits expire?**
Referral credits are valid for 12 months from the date they're issued.
