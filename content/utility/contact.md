# Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I book my first appointment?**

You can book online for most services, or call us to discuss your needs and we'll guide you to the right service. If you're unsure which service is right for you, we recommend calling — our care coordinators can help you choose.

**What should I bring to my appointment?**

Please bring a list of current medications and supplements, any relevant medical records or previous test results, and your questions. If you're a member, your health records are already in our system.

**How long will my appointment take?**

Initial consultations typically last 45-90 minutes depending on the service. We'll confirm the expected duration when you book. Follow-up appointments are usually shorter.

**What if I need to cancel?**

We understand that plans change. Please give us as much notice as possible — cancellations with more than 48 hours' notice receive a full refund. See our [Terms & Conditions](/terms-and-conditions/) for full details.
