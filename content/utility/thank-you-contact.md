## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long before I hear back?**
Our Client Manager will contact you within 1 working day to discuss your needs. If we contact you by phone and reach voicemail, we'll leave a message and follow up by email.

**What information should I have ready?**
Have details of your case or inquiry ready (case type, timeline, specific issues). This helps us suggest the right expert quickly.

**What if I don't hear back within 24 hours?**
Please check your spam folder (our emails sometimes get filtered). If you still don't see a message, call us at 01306 879 975 and we'll reconnect you with your Client Manager.

**Can I add more information to my inquiry?**
Yes -- reply to the email from our Client Manager or call 01306 879 975. Any additional details help us match you with the right expert.

**What's the next step after we agree a quote?**
You'll provide formal written instructions and we'll handle everything else -- scheduling, quality assurance, report delivery.
