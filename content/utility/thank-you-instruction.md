## Your expert instruction is confirmed.

We've matched you with a qualified psychologist/psychiatrist. Your Client Manager will call within 24 hours to discuss next steps and scheduling.

## What Happens Now

Your formal instruction has been received and logged. Here's the timeline:

**Today:** Your instructions are passed to your dedicated Client Manager and the matched expert.

**Within 24 hours:** Your Client Manager will call to discuss the scope of the assessment, confirm timescales, and arrange the expert's appointment. You'll receive the expert's CV and professional background.

**Within 5-10 working days:** The assessment takes place (typically 2-4 hours depending on complexity). The expert may request additional documents or information before the appointment.

**Within 4-6 weeks:** The final report is completed, quality assured, and delivered to you. Urgent cases may be expedited.

**Throughout:** Your Client Manager is your single point of contact. They'll manage all coordination, answer questions, and ensure smooth delivery.

## Your Expert & Quality Assurance

Your expert is:

- **HCPC registered** (psychology) or **GMC registered** (psychiatry)
- **Enhanced DBS checked** and on the DBS update service
- **Experienced** in your specific case type or field
- **Fully insured** with professional indemnity insurance
- **Independent** and bound by professional codes of conduct

Every report undergoes 100% quality assurance before delivery. We check for completeness, clarity, and compliance with court or regulatory requirements.

## Questions About Your Instruction

**When will the expert contact me?**
Your Client Manager will call within 24 hours to arrange a direct conversation with the expert or to schedule the initial appointment.

**What if I need to reschedule?**
Let your Client Manager know as soon as possible. We'll work with the expert to find an alternative date. Note: cancellations with less than 3 working days' notice may incur a fee.

**How do I prepare for the assessment?**
Your expert will outline what documents or information they need before the appointment.

**Can I request changes to the expert?**
Yes, though it's rare. If you have concerns after instruction, contact your Client Manager immediately. We'll discuss alternatives if the match isn't right.

**What happens after the report is delivered?**
The report is yours to use as intended. For legal cases, you can present it to court. For school assessments, you can use it for EHCP processes or intervention planning.

**What if I have questions during the assessment process?**
Contact your Client Manager -- that's exactly what they're there for. They're your single point of contact.
