# Medical Care

## NHS Services

It is vital that you attend all routine NHS appointments. Our services are offered in addition to, not as a replacement for, NHS care.

## Ultrasound Safety

Ultrasound is a screening tool — it cannot detect all abnormalities. If an abnormality is detected, your practitioner will discuss findings fully and provide a report for your healthcare provider.

## Internal Examinations

In some circumstances, particularly during early pregnancy and fertility scans, we may need to complete a transvaginal (internal) scan. This will only be done with your full consent, and a trained chaperone will be available.
